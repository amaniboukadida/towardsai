What is Deep Learning

Deep learning uses artificial neural networks to perform sophisticated computations on large amounts of data. It is a type of machine learning that works based on the structure and function of the human brain. .

Deep learning algorithms train machines by learning from examples. Industries such as health care, eCommerce, entertainment, and advertising commonly use deep learning.

Deep learning models tend to perform well with large amounts of data, whereas more traditional machine learning models stop improving after a saturation point.

Over the years, with the emergence of big data and increasingly powerful computing components, power- and data-intensive Deep Learning algorithms have surpassed most other methods. They seem to be ready to solve many problems: recognizing faces, defeating go or poker players, enabling the driving of autonomous cars or even the search for cancer cells.
